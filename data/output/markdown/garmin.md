# Garmin Ltd.

## Overview
**Garmin Ltd.** is a **Large Public** firm based in **Olathe, KS**.
They perform **GPS technology, consumer fitness wearables, and health monitoring devices** for **Fitness enthusiasts, athletes, and health-conscious consumers globally**.

Garmin's wearable devices are widely used in academic research studies due to their research-grade sensor accuracy, open data access APIs, and high adoption among physically active populations — creating opportunities for UNC's exercise science, population health, and digital biomarker research programs.

**Garmin Health Enterprise Solutions / Garmin Research Partnerships** is the best avenue for partnership because Garmin Health offers an enterprise API platform specifically designed for clinical and research use, providing academic institutions with structured access to Garmin wearable data for IRB-approved studies.

## Talking Points
- **Garmin Health Enterprise API for Research:** Garmin Health provides a structured enterprise API platform that gives academic researchers direct access to wearable sensor data including heart rate, HRV, sleep, stress, and activity metrics — one of the most researcher-accessible wearable data platforms available. [1]
- **Research-Grade Sensor Accuracy:** Garmin devices are frequently validated in peer-reviewed research for accuracy in heart rate, VO2 max estimation, and sleep staging — making them a preferred wearable platform for academic studies requiring validated physiological measurement. [2]
- **Exercise and Physical Activity Research:** Garmin's dominant market position among fitness and athletic populations creates a research resource for studying physical activity, athletic performance, recovery, and chronic disease prevention aligned with UNC's exercise science and physical activity epidemiology programs. [3]
- **All-Day Health Monitoring:** Garmin's 24/7 physiological monitoring including pulse oximetry, HRV, body battery, and respiration rate creates longitudinal data streams applicable to UNC research on sleep, stress, and cardiometabolic health monitoring. [4]
- **Population Health and Workforce Wellness:** Garmin Health has partnered with employers and health plans on workforce wellness programs, creating a research environment for studying wearable-based workplace health promotion that aligns with UNC's occupational health research programs. [5]
